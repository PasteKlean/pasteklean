How it works

Clean paste in three steps

PasteKlean lives in your system tray and watches your clipboard. The moment you copy, it can clean — no extra clicks.

01

Copy anything

Copy text from a web page, email, document, or code snippet — messy formatting and all.

02

PasteKlean cleans

The app reads your clipboard, applies the rules you enabled, and writes the cleaned text back.

03

Paste confidently

Paste anywhere and get plain, predictable text without hidden markup or weird spacing.

Features

Everything your clipboard needs

Toggle each cleaning rule independently. Use the app manually, automatically, or with a global shortcut.

Automatic cleaning

Enable auto-clean and let PasteKlean sanitize every copy in real time, so your clipboard is always ready.

Powerful formatting rules

Trim whitespace, collapse spaces, normalize line endings, strip HTML tags, and remove non-ASCII characters.

Global shortcut

Press Ctrl+Shift+C (or set your own) to clean the clipboard instantly from anywhere.

Private by design

No servers, no analytics, no accounts. Your clipboard never leaves your machine.

Your clipboard. Your data. Your control.

PasteKlean is built privacy-first. All cleaning happens locally on your device. There are no external servers, no usage analytics, and no advertising trackers.
