## Full Description

**PasteKlean** is the fastest way to clean up text before you paste it.

No giant dashboard. No network calls. No extra clicks. Just a quiet, lightweight system-tray app that lives in the background and automatically cleans your clipboard the moment you copy something.

### What it does

PasteKlean runs in the background and monitors your clipboard. The instant it detects copied text, it can:

- **Trim leading and trailing whitespace** — remove accidental spaces from the start and end of copied text.
- **Normalize line endings** — convert Windows and old Mac line breaks into clean, consistent newlines.
- **Collapse multiple spaces** — turn runs of tabs and spaces into a single space.
- **Remove empty lines** — strip extra blank lines when you need a compact result.
- **Strip HTML tags** — remove leftover HTML markup from copied web content.
- **Remove non-ASCII characters** — keep only plain, portable text.
- **Replace smart quotes** — convert curly quotes and dashes into straight ASCII characters.

### Built for focus

- **Auto-clean** — set it once and let it clean every copy automatically.
- **Manual clean** — press a single global keyboard shortcut or click the tray icon.
- **Preferences window** — simple toggles to choose exactly which cleaning rules you want.
- **Notifications** — optional, subtle toasts when your clipboard is cleaned.
- **Open at login** — start with Windows and forget it is there.

### Privacy first

PasteKlean is **zero-backend**. Your clipboard never leaves your machine. There are no servers, no analytics, and no account required.

### Perfect for

- Copying text from web pages into emails or documents.
- Cleaning code snippets before sharing them.
- Preparing plain-text quotes from formatted sources.
- Anyone who wants the formatting removed before they paste.

Download PasteKlean today and never paste messy text again.
